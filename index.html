Top three activites to do in Buffalo

Visit Niagra Falls

Enjoy the beautiful views, boat ride around the falls, and even a jet-boat ride in the rapids!

Try Buffalo Wings

Duff's and Anchor Bar are the two big names for wings. Opt for blue cheese instead of ranch to fit in with the locals.

Attend a Bill's Game

Get ready to SHOUT Go Bills and don't forget to break a table at the tailgate for the true Buffalo experience!
